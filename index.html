About the Event

Igniting innovation for the defence and space ecosystem

India is entering a new era, where entrepreneurial spirit fuels rockets, and innovation safeguards nations. E-Summit 2026 by the Institute Innovation Council (IIC) at IIT ISM Dhanbad focuses on defence and space, bringing together visionaries, innovators, and changemakers.

Set to unfold on 6th, 7th and 8th August 2026, this three-day summit will spotlight cutting-edge technologies, strategic entrepreneurship, and groundbreaking ideas that will reshape the future of aerospace, security, surveillance, and national resilience.

The summit is designed to empower students and professionals to build ventures, solve real-world challenges, and connect with mentors, investors, and industry experts. Expect intensive competitions, live simulations, and deep-dive conversations across emerging domains.

Join us to accelerate your ideas, sharpen your strategy, and be part of the next wave of entrepreneurs driving national impact .
